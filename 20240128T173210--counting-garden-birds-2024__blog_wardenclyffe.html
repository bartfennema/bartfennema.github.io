Counting Garden Birds 2024

So today we went to our allottment garden and for half an hour we counted all birds we saw.

It is part of a Dutch thing called "Tuinvogeltelling". You participate by doing this for half an hour , this weekend, and uploading your count to the website of "Vogelbescherming". This is a Dutch organisation that protects birds in the Netherlands. The probably do a lot more..

Anyhoo, here's what I counted(in Dutch):

Birdname count
Duif 28
Grasparkiet 6
Ekster 4
Aalscholver 3
Meeuw 23
Kraai 6
Kauw 1
Koolmeesjes 12
Reiger 1

See you next year!
